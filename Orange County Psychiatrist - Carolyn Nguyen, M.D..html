About Dr. Carolyn
 Dr. Carolyn Nguyen is a double board-certified Adult and Child Psychiatrist in Orange County, CA. She received a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) degree in Biology at Harvard University in Cambridge, MA. After obtaining her medical degree (M.D.) at UC Davis School of Medicine in Davis, CA, she went on to complete her medical internship at Harbor-UCLA Medical Center in Torrance, CA. She finished her residency training in Adult Psychiatry and additional subspecialty fellowship training in Child & Adolescent Psychiatry at UCLA Semel Institute (UCLA Neuropsychiatric Hospital and UCLA Ronald Reagan Medical Center) in Los Angeles, CA. She earned ABPN Board Certification in General Adult Psychiatry as well as Child & Adolescent Psychiatry.

During her training, she held several leadership positions in organized psychiatry at the state and national level, and also received numerous awards and honors, including the American Psychiatric Association/Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration's "Minority Fellowship Award", the Southern California Psychiatric Society's "Outstanding Resident Award", and the American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry "Outstanding Child And Adolescent Psychiatry Resident Award". She completed 3 terms on the Orange County Mental Health Board as a Professional Member and currently serves as a Councillor for the Executive Council in the Orange County Psychiatric Society. She is a Distinguished Fellow (DFAPA) of the American Psychiatric Association and Member of the American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry.

She has a private practice that specializes in treating children, adolescents, adults, and families who are experiencing emotional, behavioral or relationship difficulties. She provides services in diagnostic evaluations, consultations, medication treatment (psychopharmacology), and therapy (dynamic psychotherapy, couples therapy, family therapy, cognitive behavioral therapy, parent training, etc.). She aims to provide an integrative biopsychosocial approach and personalized care to each individual and family with her philosophy of trying to minimize medications when possible but also being comfortable to use medications effectively when necessary. Her interests include ADHD, Anxiety Disorders (OCD, GAD, Panic Disorder, etc.), Mood and Psychotic Disorders, as well as Advocacy and Cross-Cultural Psychiatry. She particularly enjoys dynamic psychotherapy with adolescents and adults.

Insurance
Dr. Carolyn Nguyen does not accept Medi-Cal or Medicare and is not on any insurance panels. However, she is considered an out-of-network physician for PPO insurance plans and can provide you with a "superbill" statement that you can submit to your insurance company for reimbursement of a portion of your paid fee (varies from 20% to 90% reimbursement). Please contact your insurance company for details on whether they reimburse policy holders for out-of-pocket payment for the following psychiatric services codes rendered by noncontracted providers: 90792 (initial psychiatric consultation); 99214 (evaluation and ~EM~ - moderate); +90833 (30 min psychotherapy w/EM); +90836 (45 min psychotherapy w/EM); +90838 (60 min psychotherapy w/EM); +90785 (interactive complexity); 90832 (30 min psychotherapy); 90834 (45 min psychotherapy); 90837 (60 min psychotherapy); 90846 or 90847 (family or couples therapy).

Dr. Carolyn Nguyen wants to provide high-quality services that are tailored to your individual circumstances and personal needs, rather than based on an insurance company's demands and restrictions upon the time and frequency she spends on your care. Elimination of paperwork and hours on the phone with insurance companies allows her to spend more quality time with you.
